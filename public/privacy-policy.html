Privacy Policy

How VK Global handles the limited information collected through this website.

This Privacy Policy describes how VK Global Investment & Technology Development JSC ("we", "us") handles information you provide through this website. We respect your privacy and only collect what is necessary to respond to inquiries.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you voluntarily submit through the Contact form, including your full name, email address, phone number (optional), company name (optional), the inquiry type and the message content.

2. How we use information

  • To respond to product, logistics and business inquiries.
  • To prepare quotations and clarify technical requirements.
  • To follow up on previous inquiries when appropriate.

3. Cart data and local storage

The inquiry cart is stored entirely in your browser using localStorage. We do not upload your cart contents to any server until you submit them through the Contact form. Clearing your browser storage will remove the cart.

4. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. A simple consent banner is shown on your first visit so we can remember your local-storage preference. Your choice is stored in your browser only.

5. Payment information

We do not collect any payment information through this website. There is no checkout page, no card form and no PayPal login. All commercial transactions are arranged offline directly with our trade desk.

6. No backend database

This website is a static informational platform. It does not maintain a user account system or a backend database of visitors. Form submissions are sent directly to our trade desk for human handling.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your contact information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with our logistics or mill partners strictly when necessary to fulfil your inquiry.

8. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of or deletion of any personal information you have shared with us by contacting info@vkglobal.vn.

9. Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version is always available on this page.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related question, please contact us at info@vkglobal.vn.
