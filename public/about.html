VK Global Investment & Technology Development JSC is a multidisciplinary business operating across logistics coordination, technology investment and international stainless steel trading.

Our story

A partner for projects that need certainty.

We were founded to bridge a gap that international buyers and project owners face every day: reliable sourcing of certified stainless steel, predictable logistics, and the technology to keep both transparent.

Today we serve fabricators, EPC contractors, OEM manufacturers and trading partners with a sharp focus on quality, traceability and on-time delivery.

What we focus on

  • Logistics coordination across global trade lanes
  • Technology investment in industrial & supply-chain ventures
  • International trade of stainless steel products
  • Sourcing & supply of certified stainless steel
  • Business support and trade communication
  • Professional product inquiry handling
